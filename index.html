About us

Welcome to Lonar's Soft Comfort.

We are your trusted local supplier of premium hygiene products. Launched in February 2026, Lonar's Soft Comfort is a proud kasi business dedicated to bringing comfort, quality and affordability directly to your doorstep.

Our story

We noticed that families in our community often had to travel far to shopping centers or settle for low-quality products just to find everyday essentials within their budget. Lonar's Soft Comfort was started to change that.

We supply locally packaged, premium-quality toilet paper in convenient bundle sizes including 4, 9, 16, 24 and 48 packs, tailored to suit your family's needs and wallet. Looking ahead, our goal is to expand into high-quality household detergents, making us your one-stop shop for home hygiene.

Why choose us?

  • Premium Quality: Soft, strong tissue that gives your family the comfort they deserve.
  • Kasi Prices: We keep our costs low so we can pass the savings directly on to you.
  • Unmatched Convenience: No need to carry heavy bundles from distant shops. We offer free delivery within Alexandra Township and clear delivery fees for wider areas.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: Bring the Comfort Home. We want every household in our community to have easy access to essential hygiene products without compromising on quality or breaking the bank.
